Our Story

Velvet Vogue was founded in 2020 by John Finlo, an ambitious entrepreneur with a passion for fashion and a vision to make premium clothing accessible to young adults. What started as a small boutique has grown into a thriving online destination for trendy casualwear and elegant formal wear.

We believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it's a form of self-expression. Our curated collections are designed to help you discover your unique style and feel confident in every occasion, whether you're heading to the office, a casual night out, or a special event.

Today, thousands of satisfied customers trust Velvet Vogue for their fashion needs, and we're committed to continuing this journey of growth and excellence.
