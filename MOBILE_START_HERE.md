## Quick Test

### On Your Phone
1. Open your site on a mobile device
2. All buttons should be easily tappable
3. Text should be readable without zooming
4. No horizontal scrolling
5. Forms should be easy to fill

### In Browser DevTools
1. Open DevTools (F12)
2. Click "Toggle device toolbar"
3. Test at 375px (iPhone size)
4. Test at 768px (iPad size)
5. Test at 1024px (desktop size)
